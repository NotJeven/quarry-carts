About

Quarry Carts is a simple escort/payload Minecraft PvP map. Two teams battle to escort their minecart payload to the end of their track. The first team to reach the end of their track with their payload wins. Players are outfited with protective armor, a sword, a bow, a shovel, and a pile of dirt to manage their defences and offensive pushes.
